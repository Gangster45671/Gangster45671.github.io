Impacts and the Significance of the Internet

Positive Impacts

  • Communication
    • You can communicate in real time, from anywhere.
    • Live streams, can be watched from anywhere by thousands of people at the same time.
  • Information
    • You can search for anything, and get results within seconds.
    • If a library contains thousands of books, the internet contains millions of websites. There is information and work on everything.
  • Buisness/Services
    • You can now shop online, and get goods delivered to your doorstep.
    • Any company big or small can have a website as part of their publicity. Somewhere where they can advertise, or inform about their services.
    • Entire web based companies exist, such as YouTube, MediaFire, Wikipedia...
  • Social Media
    • You can find childhood friends and family and talk to them in social media. Such as Facebook.
    • You can be helped to find job wih social media, with sites such as LinkedIn.
    • You can join communities, involving things you like, to talk about them. Like Reddit.
  • Media/News
    • Most newspapers today don't print papers as much as they used to or at all. They just publish their news online. With the bonus of being instant.

Negative Impacts

  • Privacy
    • It is now extremely easy to publish your life to the internet. And for random strangers to know you. We give your privacy away.
    • We openly give personal information away, some companies can take this and sell it, or use it to show us personalized ads.
  • Theft
    • The internet is full of scams. If you don't know any better and click on deceptive ads, you could be fooled into giving money away.
    • Not all website on the internet are ledgit, there are many fake services. That will take your money, keep it, and do nothing in return.
  • Marketing
    • It has gotten easier to access buisnesses, but it's also gotten easier for them to access you.
    • Some companies will use abusive advertising, such as adds that open popup windows, or take personal information.
  • Communication
    • You can talk to anyone, but anyone can talk to you.
    • It is easy to talk to strangers.
  • Information
    • As said above, your information can be tossed around in the internet and used against you.
    • Information stays. Putting it on the internet is easy, but removing it can be impossible.

Thanks for reading/watching/doing.
