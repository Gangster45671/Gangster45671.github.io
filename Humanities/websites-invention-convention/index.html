But how does it acctually work? How does information travel trough computers around the world?

First off the different systems need to be connected to eachother forming a hiearchy. One that stretches from your computer, through a local area network(LAN), a Internet Service Provider(ISP), a national service provider(NSP) and then reverse.


However, there were two ways developed to connect to your ISP's backbone. Initially you had to connect through a modem to the telephone lines, but now through WiFi or Ethernet you can connect to a LAN.
